## 🏛️ Our Story
AllUs was founded in 2022 by **Jacob Rivera**, then a freshman at a STEM-specialized high school in Puerto Rico. The original goal was to make college advice accessible for thousands of students from underserved communities who were unsure which career path was right for them. 

What started as a local initiative soon expanded into the digital world with our first student cohorts. Today, AllUs has successfully expanded its reach across two schools in East Puerto Rico, continuing our mission of equity in education.

---

## 📚 Our Mission
Providing free 1:1 STEM support and educational resources to students in underserved communities.

## 🏆 Our Success
We are proud of the community we have built and the tangible impact we have made:
